Tactical Advertising

Each week, the famous discount retailer ALDI broadcasts radio spots in Belgium and in Luxembourg. Among these are what are called "tacticals" which aim to announce an upcoming or ongoing discount.

Execution

All these radio spots are made of a jingle, a nice catch phrase as I liked too call it, a voiceover for the discount itself and a baseline. 15 seconds. I was asked to come up with a French version for them. I especially liked this new exercice as I started with the ServicePlan agency. During the 3 months I worked on this project, I kept improving and made my "catchline" more and more catchy - well duh - and just rolling off the tongue!

- realised within the ServicePlan Belgium agency, in collaboration with SonHouse Production
