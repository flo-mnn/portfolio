Context

A whole new branding and always open-minded. ALDI likes new and not so conventional ideas too! Whether it is for a top topical/event on social media or even for a PR-stunt!
What do you do when you need new ideas? You brainstorm in teams! With 2 junior art directors, we came up with nice ideas to propose to ALDI.

An April Fool Post

First, is an april fool post. Behind it was a very simple idea: displaying a ticket as if it was a fish with a headline which works in French and in Dutch, refering to the april fool's fish - as we call it in Belgium. Challenge complete. The message fits perfectly with ALDI's discount DNA.
"This is not an april fool's fish, our prices really are low"
But what is an idea without the realisation? Thanks to the senior art director Bruno Hansen, the "fish-ticket" was brought to life with this subtle hook.

A PR stunt you said?

Among other ideas we had on our brainstorming day, I present here 2 of them.
1. A "the price is right" live show event on social media to give the very same people who have no other choice but to look at all prices to actually get a bit of joy and gifts out ouf it.
2. A "kids happy moment" day. A free-to-participate moment dedicated to the kids when they can just take whatever they want, for free.
