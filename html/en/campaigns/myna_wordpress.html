The Project

Manon & Marie wished to launch their own audiovisual translation agency, specialized in subtitling.

For the digital part of their business, they needed a little guidance to get started. They already had their visual identity, logo, personas, etc. and some ideas for their website as well.

We discussed their content strategy for LinkedIn and the kind of blog articles they could write such as subtitling tools reviews for example.

A WordPress website

They wanted to start with their website so I designed what their home page could look like in Figma before setting up their WordPress website for them:

  • installing WP on their webhost
  • preparing their WP environment with a theme child
  • changing the visual identity and fonts to match their brand guidelines
  • customizing their header and footer to their needs
  • adding various plug-ins such as Yoast SEO
  • setting up Polylang for localization
  • puting the website in maintenance mode with a « we are coming » welcome page as they wanted to design and build the rest of the pages themselves

As far as I am aware, their project is unfortunately still on hold due to personal & professional reasons.
