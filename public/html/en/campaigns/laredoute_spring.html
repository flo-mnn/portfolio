TV Commercial - Welcoming Spring

La Redoute

Video Ad
February 2022

Task

Well, you know how "Spring Cleaning" is a real thing, right? Definitely not an occasion the famous online French retailer La Redoute would miss to advertise their furniture and home collection for this new season. With the team at ServicePlan Belgium agency, we got to brainstorm on Supers copys to match a 6 to 15 seconds video motion that would be broadcast on TV as well as on the internet. One of the main themes were then of course : winter is over, it's time for spring now.

Execution

This headline was presented to the client along others. The client went for a more generic - and sassy! - one that the team proposed. [Curious to know which one, huh? You can always reach out to me]
