Girleek x Billy Bike

Digital Marketing
December 2021

Task

As I enrolled in Girleek Digital Marketing training, we were assigned to work in groups on launching a new (fictitious) offer for a real brand. We agreed to work on Billy Bike, a young mobility start-up offering freefloating e-bike services in Brussels.

Launching a new offer for them was a great way to cover all important topics related to digital marketing such as: analysing the company (SWOT, 4Ps, etc), the personas, creating the offer, funneling, content strategy, content creation, landing page and website, SEO & SEA, social media, video, emailing and also KPIs and analytics.

The Offer

We imagined for Billy Bike a new offer targeting students with a nice discount during the day and 5€ price for the night, a time when bikes are less used and not profitable but also a time when students are most in need of an easy, cheap and fast way to go home.

A Phygital Campaign

For this new offer for students, we imagined a 1-month phygital campaign that would start around the Mobility Week in Brussels, which is conveniently around the start of the year for university students. Campaign would start with events on different campuses giving the opportunity for students to try out Billy bikes and participate in fun activities and quizzes.

For the campaign, these events are also a great way to gain subscribers on Billy's social media (Facebook & Instagram) as well as gather video and photo content for the digital campaign ahead.

Let's Get Social

As for the organic content, 4 categories were designed:

  • campus event stories in real-time
  • “Did you know it?” Stories and carousels on the environment and mobility
  • “Street interviews” - 1 fun question a week into a video
  • “Who's faster [metro] or Billy? Dare a friend to race you” video post and user generated content reposts
